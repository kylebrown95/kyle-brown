About me

I am a senior at the University of Missouri-Columbia graduating in December 2016. During my time at Mizzou I have worked as a copy editor, page designer and public safety and health reporter at the Columbia Missourian. I spent a semester in 2015 studying abroad in Barcelona, where I also worked as an intern for Catalan News Agency. After I graduate I plan to pursue a master's degree in journalism. My career ambition is to work as a copy editor, page designer or a hybrid of both. I'm very interested in web design as well, and I coded and designed this website myself.
